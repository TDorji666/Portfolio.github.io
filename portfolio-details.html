Project information

  • Category: App design
  • Project date: October, 2022

Product in Detail

A patisserie is a type of Italian, French or Belgian bakery that specializes in pastries and sweets, as well as a term for such food items establishment. I have designed an app to making ordering patisserie smooth

Designer Role

UX designer designing an app for the patisserie

Responsibilities

Conducting interviews, paper and digital wireframing, low and high-fidelity prototyping, conducting usability studies, accounting for accessibility, and iterating on designs.

The Problem

People are not understanding the french terms used in menus and the letters in menus are too small to be seen by people with visual problems

Project Goal

Design an app for that allows users to easily order pastries

User Research - Summary

I conducted interviews and created empathy maps to understand the users Iâ€™m designing for and their needs. A primary user group identified through research was working adults who do not have time to cook meals but research also revealed that time was not the only factor limiting users from cooking at home. Other user problems included obligations, interests, or challenges that make it difficult to get groceries for cooking or go to restaurants in-person.

Pain Points

Language Barrier

Not all users understand the french terms

Accessibility

Platforms for ordering food are not equipped with assistive technologies

Information Architecture

Text-heavy menus in apps are often difficult to read and order from

Persona : Dophu

Problem Statement

Dophu is pastry lover in France who wants a description of the pastries he ordered and want to know the pastry he ordered since he does not understand French.
